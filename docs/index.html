Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 Aug 2024

Welcome to KiddoGuard, developed by Monkpad Apps. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines how KiddoGuard handles your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

KiddoGuard does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not transmit any information to the internet or any third-party services.

2. Permissions Used by KiddoGuard

KiddoGuard requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Allows the app to manage access to external storage for certain functionalities, but no data is collected or transmitted.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Allows the app to read data from external storage, but this information is not collected or shared.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: Allows the app to access the list of installed apps on your device to provide the core functionality of KiddoGuard. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

3. Data Security

KiddoGuard operates entirely offline. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of unauthorized access to your information. Your data remains private and secure on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

KiddoGuard is designed for parents to manage the apps accessible to their children. As no data is collected, we do not require or process any personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of the app after the changes take effect will constitute your acceptance of the revised policy.
